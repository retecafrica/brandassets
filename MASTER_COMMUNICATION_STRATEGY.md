## üö® CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Negative Feedback on Social Media:

**1. Respond Quickly** (<1 hour if possible)

**2. Acknowledge Publicly**
```
"Thanks for the feedback. We're sorry you had this experience. Can we DM to understand better and make it right?"
```

**3. Solve Privately**
- Move to DM immediately
- Listen to full concern
- Offer solution or explanation
- Follow up to ensure satisfaction

**4. Learn and Improve**
- Document the issue
- Fix if it's a real problem
- Update messaging if it's a misunderstanding

### If School Has Bad Demo Experience:

**1. Apologize Sincerely**
```
"I'm really sorry the demo didn't go well. That's not the experience we want for any school. Can you tell me what went wrong?"
```

**2. Offer Make-Good**
- Schedule new demo with senior team member
- Extended trial period
- Extra support resources
- Whatever makes sense

**3. Learn**
- What went wrong?
- Technical issue or expectation mismatch?
- How can we prevent this?
