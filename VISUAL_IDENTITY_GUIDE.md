## ğŸ¨ BRAND DO'S AND DON'TS

### Visual Do's âœ…
- Keep designs simple and clean
- Use brand colors consistently
- Ensure text is readable (high contrast)
- Leave white space (don't cram everything)
- Maintain consistent logo placement
- Use high-quality images (minimum 1080px wide)

### Visual Don'ts âŒ
- Don't use random fonts (stick to 2-3 fonts max)
- Don't mix too many colors (Navy + Yellow + one accent)
- Don't use low-resolution images (pixelated/blurry)
- Don't change logo style frequently
- Don't use clipart (looks unprofessional)
- Don't overcomplicate designs
